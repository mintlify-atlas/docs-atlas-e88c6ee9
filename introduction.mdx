## What is Anchor?

Anchor is a **self-hosted note-taking application** that works fully offline. Notes are stored in a local database on each device, remain editable without an internet connection, and sync across devices automatically whenever connectivity is restored.

Anchor is designed for individuals and teams who want:

- **Privacy** — your notes live on your own server, never a third-party cloud
- **Reliability** — offline-first means the app works regardless of connectivity
- **Simplicity** — a clean interface focused on writing, not feature bloat
- **Flexibility** — self-host with a single Docker container, with or without an external database
